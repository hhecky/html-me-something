Lake Kabetogema 2015 & 2016 & 2017

(cab-a-tow-ga-ma)

Family Vacations to Lake Kabetogama, Minnesota.
We stay at a camp called Northern Lights Resort & Outitters ® within Voyageurs National Park on the northern most US border with Canada. The camp has several cabins right on the water and weather in the 70s. The perfect place to get away from our hot St. Louis weather for a week and fish, fish, fish. We're on the hunt for a monster walleye!
